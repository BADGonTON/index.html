Introduction

Honey Badger (BADG) is a meme token created on the TON blockchain. Inspired by the fearless honey badger, this token represents a fun and resilient community dedicated to humor, memes, and DeFi experimentation.

BADG aims to build a decentralized community driven by meme culture and creative engagement. It is a token for fun, participation, and exploration of the TON blockchain.
